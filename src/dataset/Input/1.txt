# Protocol 1

### Knowledge

A: A, B, Kab, M
B: A, B, Kab

where Kab is a shared symmetric key

### Actions

A → B : N
B → A : senc(N, Kab)
A → B : senc(M, Kab)
where N is freshly generated by A

### Property

Freshness of M
lemma freshness:
"not Ex party mess #t1 #t2 . FreshTerm(party, mess)@#t1 & FreshTerm(party, mess)@#t2 & #t1 < #t2"
